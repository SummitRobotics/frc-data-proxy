Privacy Policy

This service ("FRC Data Proxy") is used to retrieve publicly available FIRST Robotics Competition data from Statbotics and related public APIs.

This service does not collect, store, or share any personal data. No user-identifying information is logged or retained.

Requests are proxied only to publicly accessible robotics competition data sources and returned directly to the requesting client.

This service is provided for educational and competitive analysis purposes only.
